Camel

  1. Camels are quite unique, and somewhat rare animals.
  2. Camels can live without food and water for a long time, considering they live in deserts.
  3. Camels store food in their humps, and have eyelashes to protect from sand.

Chameleons

  • Chameleons are repltiles that are a part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is the most important part of communication for a chameleon.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around trees.

Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Teddy Bear

  • Bears are extremely strong animals, found almost everywhere in the world.
  • Bears are omnivorous, and it is dangerous for humans to be around them.
  • Bears are mainly of 3 types: Brown, Black and Polar.

Panda

  1. Pandas are cute animals, that are usually native to China and other Asian countries/.
  2. Pandas usually feed on bamboo. They are usually vegetarian.
  3. Pandas unique skin patterns protect them from the sun and other natural elements.

Cicada

  • Cicadas are extremely intriguing insects.
  • Cicadas usually live underground, and come out every 2-17 years depending upon the species.
  • Cicadas make quite pleasant music, which is used to repel birds.
